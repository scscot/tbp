What Arbonne Sells (in plain English)

Founded in 1975 by Norwegian entrepreneur Petter Morck and based in Irvine, California, Arbonne is a premium health and wellness company specializing in vegan, gluten-free, and cruelty-free products. The flagship skincare line is RE9 Advanced, a six-piece anti-aging system priced at $327 featuring cleanser, toner, serum, day cream, night cream, and eye cream—all free of parabens, phthalates, and synthetic fragrances. Beyond RE9, Arbonne offers nutrition products, makeup, body care, and wellness supplements. All products are PETA-certified vegan and cruelty-free, positioning Arbonne as a leader in clean beauty and plant-based wellness. The company operates through Independent Consultants who earn retail profit and build teams for additional income.

The clean beauty movement and vegan positioning are central to Arbonne's brand identity—this attracts health-conscious, eco-aware consumers and consultants passionate about toxin-free living.

Who Buys Arbonne

Arbonne customers are predominantly health-conscious women seeking clean, vegan beauty and wellness products. Customer segments include clean beauty advocates attracted to PETA certification and toxin-free formulations, anti-aging skincare users seeking premium RE9 Advanced results, wellness enthusiasts using Arbonne nutrition products, and eco-conscious consumers valuing sustainability commitments. The business opportunity attracts women seeking flexible income, particularly those passionate about clean living, plant-based wellness, and empowerment through entrepreneurship.

The premium price point positions Arbonne as aspirational—customers buy into the clean beauty lifestyle, not just individual products.

How Arbonne Distributes

Arbonne uses a direct selling multi-level marketing model where Independent Consultants earn retail profit on product sales and commissions on team volume. Consultants with exceptional performance can advance to Regional or National Vice President, earning generous cash bonuses including Mercedes Benz vehicle programs. The compensation plan rewards both personal sales and team building, with monthly bonuses available at all levels from Consultant to National Vice President. Entry-level consultants earn retail margins, while leaders earn overrides on organizational volume.

Key insight: Duplication happens through the 30 Days to Healthy Living program and RE9 skincare consultations—repeatable systems that create customer loyalty and business builder pipelines.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Arbonne emphasizes product education, particularly the 30 Days to Healthy Living nutrition program and RE9 Advanced skincare consultations. New consultants learn clean beauty talking points, vegan product benefits, social selling strategies, and team building through duplication. Corporate training includes online resources, regional events, and annual conferences. The challenge: traditional Arbonne training focuses on product passion and personal testimonials—systematic digital follow-up, automated nurture campaigns, and prospect management tools are often underdeveloped.

Getting Started with Arbonne: Your First 30 Days

  1. Day 1-3: Foundation Setup — Complete your Independent Consultant enrollment, order your Preferred Client Welcome Set (typically includes RE9 Advanced skincare samples, Arbonne Essentials protein shakes, and Fizz Sticks), and set up your Arbonne Back Office for order management.
  2. Week 1: Product Experience — Use Arbonne products daily for 7 days. Try the RE9 Advanced skincare regimen, drink Arbonne Essentials Daily Protein Shake for breakfast, and use Fizz Sticks for energy. Document your experience for authentic clean beauty testimonials.
  3. Week 1: Clean Beauty Education — Study Arbonne's vegan and cruelty-free certifications (PETA approved), botanical ingredient sourcing, and the harmful ingredients Arbonne avoids. Learn three key talking points about clean beauty and ingredient transparency.
  4. Week 2: 30 Days to Healthy Living Launch — Start your personal 30 Days to Healthy Living program. Follow the clean eating guide, use recommended Arbonne products, track your progress, and document how you feel. This becomes your authentic program testimonial.

  7. Week 3-4: First Preferred Clients — Convert 5-8 consultation/challenge participants to Preferred Clients on monthly autoship. Focus on RE9 regimens, protein shakes, or supplement packs based on their primary interest (skincare, weight management, energy).
  8. Week 4: First Independent Consultants — Identify 2-3 people passionate about clean beauty, wellness lifestyle, and vegan/eco-friendly products who have networks of like-minded individuals. Walk them through consultant enrollment and help them order their Welcome Set.
