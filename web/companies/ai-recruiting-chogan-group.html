What Chogan Group Sells (in plain English)

Chogan Group is an Italian direct sales company specializing in perfumes, cosmetics, and home care products with a catalog-based business model. The flagship offering is perfume inspired by luxury designer fragrances at accessible prices—each numbered scent corresponds to a high-end equivalent (e.g., Chogan 061 for those who love Chanel No. 5). The product line includes men's and women's fragrances, body care, makeup, skincare, wellness supplements, and home cleaning products.

The positioning centers on European luxury meets affordability. Chogan Group are made in Italy with emphasis on quality ingredients, INCI transparency, and cruelty-free formulations. The catalog model encourages sampling, scent comparison, and fragrance layering education. Consultants host mini fragrance parties, scent discovery sessions, and virtual catalog browsing events.

Key insight: Fragrance is personal and emotional. Lead with scent profiling, sampling methodology, and occasion-based bundles—not hard selling.

Markets & Footprint

Chogan Group operates primarily in Europe with strong presence in Italy, Poland, Romania, Spain, and expanding markets across Eastern Europe and select international regions. The company emphasizes catalog localization, multi-language support, and European beauty standards. Headquarters in Monopoli, Italy, with production facilities maintaining Made in Italy certification.

International reach means your scent profiles and sampling logistics must work across European markets with varying fragrance preferences.

Distribution Model

Chogan uses a binary compensation plan with matching bonuses and leadership pools. Consultants earn retail margins on direct sales, commission on team volume, and bonuses for organizational building. Ranks progress from Consultant to Diamond levels with increasing benefits and travel rewards. The low-cost starter kit includes sample sets and catalog materials.

Key insight: Sampling wins in fragrance. Give testers freely; track who tries what; follow up systematically.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Chogan emphasizes product knowledge through scent family education, fragrance layering workshops, and INCI ingredient training. New Consultants learn sampling protocols, catalog navigation, virtual party hosting, and social media content creation around scent stories. Corporate provides seasonal campaigns, promotional bundles, and new product launch kits. The challenge: managing sample distribution costs while building a systematic follow-up process that converts testers to buyers.
