What LiveGood Sells (in plain English)

LiveGood offers a membership-based wellness product line focused on affordability and transparency. The flagship products include Complete Plant-Based Protein (vanilla/chocolate), Organic Super Greens, Bio-Active Complete Multi-Vitamin, Ultra Magnesium Complex, and Organic Super Reds. Additional products cover weight management (Factor 4), sleep support (Sleep Aid), immune support (Vitamin D3-K2), and more. The core value proposition: get premium wellness products at member pricing (typically 50-70% below retail competitors) with a low monthly membership fee ($9.95). LiveGood emphasizes ingredient transparency, third-party testing, and no proprietary blends.

Key positioning: Costco for supplements—pay a small membership fee, get wholesale pricing on products you already buy.

Markets & Footprint

LiveGood operates globally with online direct-to-consumer fulfillment across North America, Europe, Latin America, and select international markets. The company leverages digital marketing heavily, with affiliates sharing side-by-side price comparisons, ingredient breakdowns, and savings calculators. The brand is particularly strong among budget-conscious wellness seekers and people already buying supplements regularly.

Global online model means your recruiting must work through mobile-first funnels and quick value demonstrations.

Distribution Model

LiveGood uses a binary compensation plan with matching bonuses and fast-start commissions. Affiliates earn through membership enrollments (retail customers and business builders), monthly residual income from team volume, and rank advancement bonuses. The company emphasizes retail customer acquisition first—building a base of monthly members who love the pricing. Monthly autoship and subscription consistency drive residual income.

Key insight: Show the math first. Side-by-side cart comparisons win faster than product feature lists.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

LiveGood emphasizes the 48-hour cart-build sprint: help new prospects identify their top 2-3 supplement gaps, build a member cart, compare pricing to their current brands (Amazon, GNC, etc.), and show the monthly savings. Affiliates are taught to use comparison tools, ingredient transparency pages, and simple referral micro-asks ("who are two friends that buy supplements?"). The challenge: converting price-motivated customers into advocates requires consistent value reinforcement and automated follow-ups.

Your AI Recruiting Playbook for LiveGood

  1. Lead with the comparison. Side-by-side member vs retail cart is the entry point. "What supplements do you take now?" opens the door to instant savings math.
  2. Two-SKU starter strategy. Begin with protein + greens or multi + magnesium. Show savings on familiar categories, upsell the full stack after week 2.
  3. Savings calculator automation. Use AI to build personalized comparison charts for each prospect's current supplement routine. Include monthly/annual savings projections.
  4. Referral micro-asks. After first win, ask for two friends who buy supplements. AI scripts the exact referral message they can copy/paste.
  5. Lean funnel design. Two-page system: value overview + checkout. AI generates the overview page with their specific cart comparison.
  6. Autoship timing optimization. Right refill window matters. AI prompts check-ins at 21 days (before they run out) with easy reorder links.
  7. Duplicate the 48-hour sprint. Save your cart-build script, comparison templates, and referral ask sequences so new affiliates can launch immediately.
