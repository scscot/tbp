What Total Life Changes Sells (in plain English)

Total Life Changes (TLC) is a health and wellness direct sales company specializing in weight management, detox products, and nutritional supplements. The flagship product is Iaso Tea, a natural cleansing and detox tea blend designed to support weight loss, digestive health, and overall wellness. The product line includes Resolution drops (weight management), NutraBurst (liquid multivitamin), IASO Gano (coffee with ganoderma), protein shakes, skincare, and CBD-infused wellness products.

The brand positions around transformation stories, before-and-after journeys, and community support systems. TLC emphasizes simple daily routines (morning tea, afternoon supplements) and social media-driven testimonials. The company uses challenge campaigns (5-day tea detox, 30-day transformation) to build momentum and create viral sharing opportunities. Products target weight loss seekers, health-conscious consumers, and business builders looking for recurring customer models.

Key insight: Results drive recruitment in weight loss. Lead with transformation tracking, photo documentation, and community accountability—not just product specs.

Markets & Footprint

Total Life Changes operates globally with presence in North America, Latin America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Africa. Headquarters in Fair Haven, Michigan, with manufacturing partnerships ensuring quality control. The company has strong social media presence and influencer partnerships driving brand awareness across diverse markets.
