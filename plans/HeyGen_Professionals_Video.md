## Production Notes

1. **Tone:** Conversational and empathetic, NOT salesy or hype-driven
2. **Emphasis Words:** "YOUR company", "YOUR link", "BEFORE they commit", "Day 1", "momentum"
3. **Pauses:** Brief pause after "They start Day 1 with momentum" for impact
4. **Avatar Expression:** Start empathetic (pain point), shift to confident (solution), end encouraging (CTA)
5. **No Image Overlays:** Video embeds on website where app screenshots are already visible
