The Journey (2009-2016)

The Team Building Project was founded in 2009 with a bold vision: to level the playing field in affiliate and network marketing by creating the first truly global platform for direct sales professionals.

72,000+
Members Worldwide
120+
Countries Represented

Within just two years, we grew to over 72,000 members from 120 countries, becoming the first global platform enabling direct sales associates to generate income across multiple countries and opportunities.

While we faced resistance from companies that restricted their associates from exploring other opportunities, we proved that network marketers wanted the freedom to build their businesses their way.

The Team Building Project closed in 2016 after seven impactful years, but the lessons learned and the vision for empowering network marketers continued to evolve.

The Next Chapter: Team Build Pro

The vision evolved into something even more powerful: an AI-powered platform for network marketers worldwide

Team Build Pro represents the evolution of everything we learned from the Team Building Project. It's a professional software tool designed specifically for network marketers who want to build their teams with the power of artificial intelligence.

The Pre-Building Advantage

Build your network foundation before joining any specific opportunity. When you're ready to launch with a company, you'll already have a thriving team in place. This is the future of network marketing.
