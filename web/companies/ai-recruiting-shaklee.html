What Shaklee Sells (in plain English)

Founded in 1956 by Dr. Forrest C. Shaklee with headquarters in Pleasanton, California, Shaklee is America's first certified Climate Neutral company with nearly 70 years of wellness innovation. The flagship product is Vivix, a premium anti-aging supplement backed by 27 global patents designed to target aging at the cellular level using polyphenol technology. Available in liquid, liquid gel, and gummy forms, Vivix is positioned as a longevity solution. Beyond Vivix, Shaklee offers Life Shake meal replacement, Life-Strip multivitamins, targeted nutrition supplements, household cleaners, and personal care products. The 2024 compensation plan allows ambassadors to earn through retail profit margins, commissions, and bonuses, with levels from Director to Presidential Master Coordinator.

The sustainability and environmental leadership story is central to Shaklee's brand identity—this attracts eco-conscious consumers and ambassadors passionate about planet-friendly business practices.

Who Buys Shaklee

Shaklee customers span wellness enthusiasts seeking premium supplements like Vivix, health-conscious consumers using Life Shake for nutrition, eco-aware buyers attracted to Climate Neutral certification, and multi-generational families loyal to Shaklee's 70-year heritage. The business opportunity attracts ambassadors seeking sustainable income through a proven compensation plan, particularly those valuing environmental responsibility and science-backed products. Shaklee's longevity creates trust—customers believe in products that have stood the test of time.

The wholesale-to-retail profit model is straightforward—ambassadors buy at wholesale and sell at suggested retail, keeping the differential.

How Shaklee Distributes

Shaklee uses a multi-level marketing compensation plan updated for 2024 that rewards ambassadors through retail profit margins, commissions on team volume, and bonuses tied to rank advancement. The main income stream is the price differential—wholesale purchase to retail sale. Ambassadors progress from Director to Presidential Master Coordinator, with higher titles unlocking more bonuses and override commissions. The plan emphasizes both personal sales and team building, rewarding duplication and leadership development across organizational levels.

Key insight: Shaklee's 70-year track record creates credibility for the business opportunity—it's not a new startup, it's a legacy company with proven systems.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Shaklee emphasizes product education, particularly Vivix's cellular anti-aging science, sustainability leadership stories, and the environmental impact of choosing Shaklee products. New ambassadors learn social selling strategies, product sampling techniques, compensation plan structure, and team building through duplication. Corporate training includes online resources, regional events, and recognition programs. The challenge: traditional Shaklee training focuses on product passion and environmental mission—systematic digital follow-up, automated prospect nurturing, and AI-powered recruiting tools are often missing.
