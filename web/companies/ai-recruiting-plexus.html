What Plexus Sells (in plain English)

Founded in 2006 by Tarl Robinson and Alfred Petterson, headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona, Plexus Worldwide specializes in weight loss supplements and gut health products. The flagship product is Plexus Slim, also known as the "Pink Drink," a weight loss supplement in powder form that turns pink when mixed with water. Plexus Slim is intended to balance blood sugar levels and restrain hunger cravings. Beyond the Pink Drink, Plexus offers multiple gut health products designed to reduce bloating and improve overall wellness, positioning gut health as central to weight management and overall vitality. To become an Ambassador and sell products while earning commission, distributors must reach Ambassador level and meet a minimum monthly order of $100.

The Pink Drink branding and gut health positioning are central to Plexus's market identity—creating memorable, shareable product messaging that stands out in the crowded wellness supplement space.

Who Buys Plexus

Plexus customers are predominantly weight loss seekers attracted to the Pink Drink's blood sugar balancing claims, gut health enthusiasts interested in bloating reduction and digestive wellness, and wellness-focused individuals seeking natural supplement solutions. The business opportunity attracts Ambassadors seeking flexible income through social selling, particularly those passionate about gut health education and weight loss transformations. However, average brand ambassador earnings were just $544 for the year in 2020 according to Plexus website disclosures, indicating modest income potential for most distributors.

The gut health angle creates a wellness positioning beyond simple weight loss—customers stay for digestive benefits even after reaching weight goals.

How Plexus Distributes

Plexus uses a multi-level marketing compensation structure offering 11 ways to make money as an independent distributor or Ambassador. The entry cost is $39.95 per year, with Ambassadors required to maintain a minimum $100 monthly order to sell products and earn commissions. Ambassadors earn on personal sales plus team commissions and bonuses. The compensation plan emphasizes both product sales and team building, though income disclosure shows average annual earnings of $544 in 2020, suggesting most Ambassadors earn modest supplemental income rather than full-time replacement income.

Key insight: Duplication happens through the Pink Drink sampling process—the visual pink color creates curiosity and shareability on social media.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Plexus emphasizes the Pink Drink product story, gut health education, weight loss testimonials, and Ambassador compensation plan opportunities. New Ambassadors learn social selling strategies, product sampling techniques, gut health talking points, and team building through duplication. Corporate training includes online resources, team events, and recognition programs. The challenge: traditional Plexus training relies heavily on personal testimonials and social media posts—systematic digital follow-up, automated gut health education sequences, and AI-powered prospect nurturing are often missing from Ambassador toolkits.

Getting Started with Plexus: Your First 30 Days

New to Plexus? Here's your step-by-step roadmap to launching your Ambassador journey with momentum:

  1. Day 1-3: Foundation Setup — Complete your Ambassador enrollment, order your Welcome Pack (typically includes Plexus Slim Pink Drink samples, product literature, and business tools), and set up your Plexus Ambassador Back Office account for order management.
  2. Week 1: Product Experience — Take Plexus Slim daily for 7 days. Mix the Pink Drink with water 30 minutes before meals, note any changes in hunger levels, energy, or digestion, and document your experience for authentic testimonials.
  3. Week 1: Gut Health Education — Study Plexus's gut health science, watch the product education videos on blood sugar balance and microbiome support, and learn three key talking points about the gut-weight connection.
  4. Week 2: Warm Market Outreach — Create your contact list of 50-100 people and segment by interest: weight management seekers, gut health/digestion issues, energy/mood support, or business opportunity. Reach out to 20 people with personalized messages.
  5. Week 2: Team Build Pro Setup — Download Team Build Pro to organize your prospect pipeline, track sample recipients, and let qualified prospects start pre-building their teams before officially joining as Ambassadors.
  6. Week 3: First Sampling Campaign — Send Pink Drink samples to 10-15 prospects with usage instructions (take 30 min before largest meal, drink plenty of water). Follow up on day 3, 7, and 14 to ask about their experience and answer questions.
  7. Week 3-4: First Customers — Convert 5-8 sample recipients to retail customers or preferred customers on monthly autoship. Focus on those who experienced noticeable benefits (reduced cravings, better digestion, steady energy).
  8. Week 4: First Ambassadors — Identify 2-3 people who loved the products and expressed interest in sharing with their networks. Walk them through Ambassador enrollment, explain the $100 monthly minimum order and annual fee, and help them order their Welcome Pack.
  9. First 30 Days: Sampling System Duplication — Package your entire Pink Drink sampling process—how to order samples, usage instructions, follow-up message templates, gut health talking points, objection responses—so new Ambassadors can launch their sampling in 72 hours.
  10. Ongoing: Consistent Momentum — Use Team Build Pro to track your team's monthly volume, maintain the $100 minimum order for Ambassador status, and identify team members ready for rank advancement based on their customer acquisition.
