What Modere Sold (in plain English)

Modere was a clean label wellness company offering over 1,000 products free from thousands of controversial chemicals and compounds. The flagship product line featured Liquid BioCell collagen supplements (Pure, Life, Beauty, Skin, and Immune formulations), along with body transformation products like Trim and the Lean Body System. Beyond supplements, Modere expanded into personal care, household cleaning solutions, and commercial-grade cleaning products—all built on the clean label philosophy.

The clean label commitment was central to the brand story—working with industry-leading scientists to combine nature's discoveries with cutting-edge innovation, creating products without harmful additives.

Markets & Footprint

Modere operated globally across six major markets: United States, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Europe, India, and Japan. The company sold over 3 million products worldwide and achieved a 1,700% revenue increase between 2016-2020, earning recognition as the fastest-growing woman-led company by the Women Presidents' Organization and JP Morgan Chase in 2021.

International reach required systems that worked across languages, cultures, and time zones—making AI-powered automation essential for global teams.

Distribution Model

Modere used a unilevel social marketing compensation plan with 8 levels of depth. Social Marketers earned through customer commissions (3-5% on customer purchases, 10-20% of Customer Points in their pod), unilevel commissions (up to 37% at Elite Social Marketer 3 rank), and Team Leader Bonuses ($250 per new Team Leader plus $250/month for consecutive months maintained). The plan featured a 35% payout rate.

Key insight: Clean beauty and wellness customers value education over hype. Consistent follow-up and product experience sharing drove duplication.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Modere emphasized product education through clean label science, ingredient transparency, and lifestyle transformation stories. New Social Marketers were encouraged to share their personal wellness journeys, host virtual product sampling events, and build through the "Experience, Share, Build" model. Corporate training included online resources, recognition programs, and regional events. The challenge: most training focused on product passion and clean label education, not systematic follow-up and AI-powered recruiting automation.

Your AI Recruiting Playbook for Modere

  1. Niche-first targeting. Segment prospects into clean beauty enthusiasts, anti-aging seekers, fitness recovery advocates, toxin-free home builders, or serious business builders. Your message and product focus shift by niche.
  2. Clean label storytelling. Lead with the "what's NOT in it" angle. Example: "Our collagen has zero artificial colors, flavors, or sweeteners—here's the ingredient transparency page."
  3. Before/after documentation system. Guide new customers through a 30-day photo and results journal for Liquid BioCell or Trim. AI prompts check-ins on days 7, 14, 21, and 30 with encouragement and reorder invitations.
  4. Educational content loops. Create repeatable 20-minute virtual sessions (Clean Label 101, Collagen Science Basics, Toxin-Free Home Swaps) with AI-generated invites, reminders, and post-event follow-ups.
  5. Sample-to-sale automation. When someone requests a Liquid BioCell sample, trigger a 14-day nurture sequence: day 1 (usage tips), day 3 (science video), day 7 (check-in), day 10 (testimonial share), day 14 (enrollment offer).
  6. Objection response library. Automate answers for: price concerns ("cost per serving vs. drugstore alternatives"), MLM skepticism ("tool for your existing wellness community"), ingredient questions ("link to third-party testing and certifications").
  7. Duplicate the entire playbook. Save your event slides, nurture sequences, FAQ responses, and niche-specific scripts so new Social Marketers can copy the system on day one—no reinventing the wheel.

Getting Started with Modere: Your First 30 Days

New to Modere? Here's your step-by-step roadmap to launching your clean label wellness social marketer journey:

  1. Day 1-3: Foundation & Clean Label Product Experience — Complete Modere Social Marketer enrollment. Study compensation plan. Order personal products from key categories—Liquid Biocell collagen (anti-aging), Trim weight management system, personal care (clean label shampoo, body wash, toothpaste), household cleaning (toxin-free formulas). Begin using products documenting experience, clean ingredient benefits, effectiveness. Personal transformation documentation becomes your credibility.
  2. Week 1: Ingredient Education & Clean Label Positioning — Study Modere's clean label science—what's NOT in products (no artificial colors, flavors, sweeteners, harsh chemicals). Learn ingredient transparency approach differentiating from conventional brands. Practice explaining clean label benefits: "Modere collagen has zero artificial ingredients—here's complete ingredient transparency" provides credible quality positioning. Focus on "Experience, Share, Build" model—personal results first, then sharing, then business building.
  3. Week 2: Niche Selection & Target Market — Choose primary product niche: clean beauty enthusiasts (toxin-free skincare), anti-aging seekers (collagen users), fitness recovery (athletes), toxin-free home builders (household products). List 20-30 prospects matching chosen niche demonstrating interest in clean ingredients, natural products, wellness optimization. Focus on people already spending on premium wellness/beauty—Modere works best repositioning existing spending toward cleaner alternatives.
  4. Week 3: Product Sampling & Results Tracking — Distribute product samples to 10-15 targeted prospects. Liquid Biocell for anti-aging interested, Trim for weight management seekers, clean personal care for toxin-avoiding families. Create results tracking tools—before/after photos for collagen, measurement tracking for Trim, ingredient satisfaction surveys for household products. Follow up Day 3, 7, 14 gathering feedback. Document positive results for testimonial use.
  5. Week 3-4: First Customer Enrollments — Convert 2-3 warmest sample recipients into Modere customers. Start with signature products showing clean label differentiation—Liquid Biocell, Trim system, personal care bundles. Explain Smart Ship autoship benefits (discounts, convenience, loyalty rewards). Set up follow-up schedules tracking ongoing product results. If entrepreneurial interest shown, explain Social Marketer opportunity: "Beyond using clean products yourself, you can earn income sharing toxin-free alternatives with others."
  6. Day 30: Master Modere AI Playbook — Implement AI tactics: niche-first targeting, clean label storytelling, sample tracking automation, educational event hosting, ingredient transparency content, lifestyle transformation documentation. Package "Experience, Share, Build" system for new Social Marketers.
  7. 30 Days Forward: Build Clean Living Authority — Continue sharing clean label education, toxin-free living tips, ingredient transparency advocacy. Position as trusted clean wellness resource. Host virtual product sampling events, ingredient education sessions, lifestyle transformation showcases. Focus recruiting on customers loving product quality who demonstrate wellness advocacy.
  8. Duplicate & Scale — Package clean label education materials, sample distribution protocols, results tracking tools, niche targeting frameworks, customer enrollment processes into turnkey system for new Social Marketers.
  9. Ongoing: Clean Label Mission Focus — Lead with ingredient transparency, toxin-free positioning, clean wellness advocacy. Your recruiting power comes from authentic lifestyle transformation and documented clean product results, not income opportunity hype.
