What LifeVantage Sells (in plain English)

LifeVantage markets science-backed nutritional supplements focused on cellular health and "biohacking." The flagship product line is Protandim—available as Nrf2 Synergizer (claims to reduce oxidative stress by 40% in 30 days), Nrf1 Synergizer (mitochondrial support), NAD Synergizer (healthy aging), and Protandim Tri-Synergizer (all three combined). Additional products include TrueScience skincare, Omega+ supplements, and Axio energy drinks. The marketing emphasizes peer-reviewed research and activation of the body's natural antioxidant production pathways.

Key positioning: Science-based biohacking for people who want cellular-level health optimization, not just symptom management.

Markets & Footprint

LifeVantage operates globally with presence in North America, Asia-Pacific (Japan, Australia, Thailand, Hong Kong), and select international markets. Headquarters in Sandy, Utah, with fulfillment centers supporting worldwide distribution.

Global reach means your recruiting must translate scientific concepts across cultures and languages.

Distribution Model

LifeVantage uses a unilevel compensation plan where Consultants earn retail profits, commissions on team volume, and bonuses. Requires 40 PV personal purchases monthly to qualify for commissions. Ranks progress from Consultant through Pro levels to Elite.

Key insight: Scientific credibility matters more than hype. Educational content and research citations win trust.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

LifeVantage emphasizes product education through clinical study summaries, peer-reviewed research links, and 30-day challenge protocols. New Consultants are encouraged to share the Nrf2 oxidative stress story, use before/after biomarker tracking (if available), and build through "Try, Share, Optimize" methodology. The challenge: translating complex science into simple, compelling messages without oversimplifying or making prohibited health claims.
