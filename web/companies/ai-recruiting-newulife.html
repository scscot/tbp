What NewULife Sells (in plain English)

The product line features ketone supplements for metabolic support and weight management, skincare products with anti-aging formulas, weight loss products, and wellness formulas targeting vitality and recovery. Somaderm is priced at $45 per bottle retail and marketed with claims of improved sleep, skin elasticity, energy levels, and recovery time. The company emphasizes transdermal delivery technology (absorption through skin) and social media-driven marketing strategies.
