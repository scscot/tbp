Everything you need to know about PreIntake.ai

PreIntake.ai provides an AI-powered chat widget that you embed on your law firm's website. When potential clients visit your site, they interact with our intelligent intake system that:

  • Engages them in a natural, conversational intake experience
  • Asks practice-specific screening questions based on your firm's criteria
  • Evaluates responses against legal qualifications (SOL, jurisdiction, etc.)
  • Routes qualified leads to your CRM or email in real-time
  • Provides plain-English rationale for each screening decision

The widget matches your firm's branding and works 24/7, screening leads before they ever reach your intake team.

PreIntake.ai supports a wide range of legal practice areas, each with its own specialized screening logic:

  • Personal Injury - Vehicle accidents, premises liability, dog bites, rideshare incidents
  • Immigration - Family-based, employment-based, asylum, VAWA, U-visa
  • Family Law - Divorce, custody, support, protective orders
  • Bankruptcy - Chapter 7, Chapter 13, means test screening
  • Criminal Defense - Misdemeanors, felonies, DUI/DWI, federal crimes
  • Tax/IRS - Disputes, audits, Offer in Compromise
  • Estate Planning - Wills, trusts, probate
  • Employment Law - Discrimination, wrongful termination, wage disputes
  • Workers' Compensation - Workplace injuries, benefits disputes
  • Real Estate - Transactions, disputes, landlord-tenant

We can also customize screening for other practice areas based on your firm's needs.

Adding PreIntake.ai to your website is simple. After setup, you receive a small code snippet that looks like this:

<script src="https://preintake.ai/widget/your-firm-id.js"></script>

You (or your web developer) paste this code into your website, typically just before the closing </body> tag. That's it—the widget appears automatically and starts screening leads immediately.

Works with any website platform: WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, custom sites, or any CMS. No special plugins or technical expertise required.
