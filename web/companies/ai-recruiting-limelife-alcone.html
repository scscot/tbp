What LimeLife by Alcone Sells (in plain English)

LimeLife by Alcone bridges professional makeup artistry with everyday consumer accessibility, built on Alcone Company's 60+ year legacy as the trusted makeup supplier for Broadway, film, television, and professional makeup artists. The product line features long-wear cosmetics engineered for stage performance durability yet wearable for daily life—Perfect Foundation (buildable coverage with skincare benefits), Enduring Lip Color (8-hour wear without feathering), Perfect Pressed Powder (HD-ready finish), Eye Got This Liner (waterproof precision), and Bestie Eye Shadow Palettes (highly pigmented, blendable formulas). The skincare collection includes Moisture Boost Serum, Eye Bright Depuffer, and Dream Team Night Cream. LimeLife emphasizes clean beauty ingredients, cruelty-free formulations, and professional-quality application tools. The company operates across eight countries (US, Canada, UK, Ireland, Germany, France, Spain, Austria) with no monthly minimums, starter kits from $29, and 20% product discounts for Beauty Guides.

LimeLife's Alcone heritage differentiates from typical direct-sales cosmetics—professional makeup artists trust Alcone for Broadway shows and film sets, bringing that same performance-tested quality to everyday consumers through the Beauty Guide model.
