Email Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Quick Summary: This privacy policy applies specifically to email communications sent from info.teambuildpro.com. We collect minimal information, never sell your data, and you can unsubscribe anytime.

1. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to our email communications, we collect and store:

  • Email Address: Required to send you emails
  • Name: Used for email personalization (if provided)
  • Subscription Date: When you subscribed to our list
  • Email Preferences: Your communication preferences and interests
  • Engagement Data: Email opens, clicks, and interaction metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email information exclusively for:

  • Sending you AI recruiting tips and strategies
  • Notifying you of product updates and new features
  • Sharing promotional offers and special discounts
  • Improving our email content based on engagement
  • Managing your subscription preferences

Email Tracking

Our emails may include tracking pixels to measure:

  • Email open rates
  • Link click-through rates
  • Device and email client usage
  • Geographic location (general region, not precise)

This data helps us understand what content is valuable to you and improve our communications.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to manage email communications:

  • Mailgun: Email delivery and delivery analytics
  • Firebase/Firestore: Secure storage of subscriber information

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and are GDPR/CCPA compliant.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase provides enterprise-grade security and encryption at rest
  • Access to subscriber data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review and update security practices

5. Data Retention

  • Active Subscribers: We retain your data as long as you remain subscribed
  • Unsubscribed: We mark you as unsubscribed but retain your email to prevent re-adding you
  • Complete Deletion: Contact us at sscott@info.teambuildpro.com to request complete deletion

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your email data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data we store
  • Correction: Update incorrect or outdated information
  • Deletion: Request complete removal from our systems
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Preferences: Manage what types of emails you receive

Exercise these rights by:

  • Unsubscribing from all emails
  • Managing preferences for specific email types
  • Contacting us at sscott@info.teambuildpro.com

7. CAN-SPAM Compliance

We comply with the CAN-SPAM Act by:

  • Never using false or misleading header information
  • Not using deceptive subject lines
  • Identifying messages as advertisements when appropriate
  • Including our physical business address in emails
  • Providing a clear and easy way to unsubscribe
  • Honoring opt-out requests within 10 business days

8. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

For users in the EU and California:

  • We process data based on legitimate interest (providing requested content)
  • You can request data portability in machine-readable format
  • You have the right to object to processing
  • We do not sell personal information to third parties
  • We do not use personal information for discriminatory purposes

9. Children's Privacy

Our email services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us their email, contact us immediately for removal.

10. International Data Transfers

Your email data may be stored on servers located in the United States. By subscribing, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this email privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued subscription after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Domain Separation

This policy applies specifically to email communications from info.teambuildpro.com. For the main Team Build Pro application and website privacy policy, visit teambuildpro.com/privacy_policy.html.

13. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or to exercise your privacy rights:

  • Email: sscott@info.teambuildpro.com
  • Unsubscribe: info.teambuildpro.com/unsubscribe.html
  • Preferences: info.teambuildpro.com/preferences.html
  • Main Website: teambuildpro.com

We Respect Your Privacy

At Team Build Pro, we believe in transparency and respect for your privacy. We only send emails you've opted into, and you can unsubscribe or adjust preferences anytime. We never sell or share your email with third parties for their marketing purposes.
