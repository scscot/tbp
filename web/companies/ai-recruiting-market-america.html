What Market America Sells (in plain English)

Market America is a product brokerage and internet marketing company that specializes in one-to-one marketing through its e-commerce platform Shop.com. The company partners with major brands and manufactures exclusive products across multiple categories including Isotonix nutritional supplements, Motives cosmetics, TLS weight management, Royal Spa personal care, and home care products. Market America acquired Shop.com in 2010 and has since awarded over $50 million in cashback rewards to customers. The business model combines traditional e-commerce with a network of UnFranchise Owners who earn commissions on product sales and build teams of distributors.

The UnFranchise concept emphasizes that this is more than retail arbitrage—it's about building a sustainable e-commerce business with residual income potential and leveraging the Shop.com marketplace as your storefront.

Who Buys Market America

Market America attracts savvy online shoppers seeking cashback rewards on everyday purchases, health-conscious consumers drawn to Isotonix's isotonic delivery system for faster nutrient absorption, beauty enthusiasts looking for Motives by Loren Ridinger cosmetics, and weight management seekers using the TLS program. The business opportunity appeals to aspiring entrepreneurs who understand e-commerce, want residual income from customer reorders, and appreciate the Partner Store concept that allows them to earn commissions on thousands of products beyond Market America's exclusive brands.

The Shop.com integration is powerful—customers can shop major retailers and your team earns cashback commissions on purchases they're making anyway.

How Market America Distributes

Market America provides UnFranchise Owners with personalized e-commerce websites called Partner Stores, access to the Shop.com platform with thousands of retail partners, and a binary compensation plan that pays commissions on product sales plus team building bonuses. The startup costs range from $149.95 for annual subscription-only to $449.95 for comprehensive Fast Start Kits. Monthly requirements include a $21.95 Unfranchise Management System fee and quarterly volume minimums of $200 retail to maintain active status and preserve accumulated commission points.

Key insight: The cashback rewards program and Partner Store model mean your customers benefit even when they shop outside your exclusive product line—creating stickiness and recurring commissions.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Market America emphasizes the UnFranchise business concept first, teaching distributors to think like e-commerce entrepreneurs rather than traditional network marketers. Training covers the Shop.com platform, Partner Store customization, cashback rewards positioning, product knowledge across multiple categories, and the binary compensation structure. Corporate provides online training portals, regional business briefings, and annual international conventions. The challenge: most training focuses on in-person presentations and hotel meetings—digital nurture sequences, automated follow-up funnels, and AI-powered prospect qualification are rarely systematized.

  3. Partner Store traffic campaigns. Build automated email and text sequences that drive traffic to prospects' Partner Stores with limited-time offers, cashback bonuses, and exclusive product bundles that create urgency and repeat purchases.
  4. Isotonic delivery storytelling. Develop a content library explaining Isotonix's isotonic delivery system for maximum nutrient absorption—AI matches scientific explanations with customer testimonials based on prospect health goals.

  6. Binary compensation visualizations. Use AI to explain Market America's binary structure with interactive tools that let prospects input scenarios and see earning projections based on team building and volume requirements.
  7. Duplicate the Partner Store setup. Package your entire Partner Store customization process, product selection strategy, cashback positioning scripts, and follow-up sequences so new UnFranchise Owners can launch in 72 hours with a proven system.
