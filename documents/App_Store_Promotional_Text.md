## A/B Testing Strategy

Since promotional text updates instantly, you can test different approaches:

1. **Week 1-2**: Current text (dual-audience, feature-focused)
2. **Week 3-4**: Pain point-focused ("75% quit in year one...")
3. **Week 5-6**: Social proof version (when you have user count)
4. **Analyze**: Compare conversion rates, downloads, trial sign-ups

**Recommendation**: Keep current text for at least 30 days to establish baseline, then test variations.

## Best Practices for Future Updates

### DO:
✅ Update for major features, milestones, or seasonal campaigns
✅ Keep it under 170 characters
✅ Maintain dual-audience messaging (professionals + prospects)
✅ Always mention 30-day free trial (key conversion driver)
✅ Use specific, concrete language (not vague marketing speak)

### DON'T:
❌ Update too frequently (causes confusion, looks unprofessional)
❌ Use all caps or excessive punctuation (!!!)
❌ Make promises you can't keep ("Get rich quick!")
❌ Forget to mention free trial
❌ Use jargon that non-MLM people won't understand
