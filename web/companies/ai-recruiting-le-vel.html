What Le-Vel Sells (in plain English)

Founded in 2012 by Jason Camper and Paul Gravette, Le-Vel created the Thrive Experience—an 8-week premium lifestyle plan designed to help individuals reach peak physical and mental performance. The three-step daily system includes Premium Lifestyle Capsules taken in the morning, a Lifestyle Shake Mix 20 minutes later, and a DFT patch applied to clean skin. The DFT technology is revolutionary—wearable nutrition that infuses the derma with Le-Vel's unique formula through extended-time dermal fusion. With over 10 million Customer and Brand Promoter accounts, Le-Vel ships to North America, Southeast Asia, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK. Products support clean weight management, energy, mental clarity, joint health, lean muscle support, and overall wellness.

The three-step ritual creates customer stickiness—people experience results from the complete system, making it harder to skip components and easier to maintain monthly subscriptions.

Who Buys Le-Vel

Le-Vel attracts busy professionals seeking sustained energy without jitters, fitness enthusiasts looking for clean weight management solutions, health-conscious consumers drawn to the wearable nutrition innovation, and wellness seekers wanting a simple daily routine for peak performance. The business opportunity appeals to entrepreneurial individuals who appreciate premium lifestyle positioning, understand subscription economics, and want to build teams around a transformational experience rather than just selling products. The 8-week Thrive Experience framework creates natural customer checkpoints for testimonials and business opportunity conversations.

The DFT patch is conversation-starting wearable tech—people notice it, ask questions, creating organic opening for product education and recruiting dialogues.
