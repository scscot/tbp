What Bravenly Global Sells (in plain English)

Bravenly Global focuses on sleep wellness and functional nutrition through lifestyle beverages and supplements. The flagship products are Gold (sleep and recovery support drink) and Rae (daytime energy and focus). Gold combines reishi mushroom, collagen, and adaptogens designed for evening wind-down routines, while Rae offers sustained energy without jitters. Additional products include Zlem (sleep support capsules), Brān (cognitive support), and Uplift (mood support). The company emphasizes ingredient transparency and taste—positioning products as swaps for existing routines rather than additions.

Key positioning: Functional wellness drinks that fit existing habits—replace your evening tea with Gold, swap your morning coffee for Rae.

Markets & Footprint

Bravenly Global operates primarily in North America (US and Canada) with direct-to-consumer fulfillment and social-selling tactics. The company leverages Instagram and TikTok heavily, with Ambassadors sharing daily routines, recipe variations (Gold lattes, Rae smoothies), and before/after sleep tracking. The brand is particularly strong among wellness influencers and busy parents seeking better sleep without prescriptions.

Social-first approach means your recruiting must work through short-form video and daily routine content.

Distribution Model

Bravenly Global uses a hybrid affiliate and network marketing compensation plan. Ambassadors earn through retail commissions on direct sales, team volume bonuses, and rank advancement incentives. The company emphasizes customer acquisition over recruiting—success comes from building a loyal retail base first, then inviting customers to become Ambassadors. Monthly autoship subscriptions drive recurring revenue.

Key insight: Taste matters more than claims. Let the product sell itself through sampling and routine swaps.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Bravenly Global emphasizes the 10-day swap challenge: new customers try Gold for 10 nights with daily photo prompts and sleep journal tracking. Ambassadors are taught to position products through habit stacking ("add Gold to your bedtime routine") and sensory storytelling (taste, texture, ritual). Training includes recipe banks, social media content templates, and team calls focused on sampling strategies. The challenge: converting samplers into autoship customers requires consistent follow-up automation.

Your AI Recruiting Playbook for Bravenly Global

  1. Lead with the swap. Don't add to routines—replace existing drinks. "What do you drink before bed?" opens better than product pitches.
  2. 10-day challenge structure. Offer guided trials with daily check-ins (photo prompts, sleep quality ratings, energy tracking). AI generates day 1 intro, day 3/5/7 nudges, day 10 decision conversation.
  3. Recipe variation library. Build a searchable collection of Gold lattes, Rae smoothie bowls, and flavor combos your team can copy. AI scripts short 15-second Reels for each.
  4. Taste-first positioning. Use AI to generate sensory descriptions ("creamy vanilla with earthy reishi notes") that beat generic wellness claims.
  5. Bundle logic automation. Good/better/best stacks (Gold solo, Gold + Rae combo, full wellness stack). AI suggests the right bundle based on prospect's goals.
  6. Sampler tracking system. When someone gets a sample, lock in a "decision day" upfront. AI reminds both parties and prompts the right follow-up question.
  7. Duplicate the 10-day flow. Save your entire challenge sequence (prompts, messages, tracking template) so new Ambassadors can launch in 24 hours.
