What 4Life Sells (in plain English)

The flagship innovation is Transfer Factor—messenger molecules extracted from cow colostrum and chicken egg yolks that educate the immune system to recognize, respond to, and remember potential health threats. Core products include Transfer Factor Plus Tri-Factor Formula (the signature immune support blend with zinc), Transfer Factor RioVida (liquid antioxidant juice), Transfer Factor Cardio (cardiovascular support), and Transfer Factor Glutamine Prime (digestive and immune health). The product line extends to targeted formulas for kids, pets, weight management (4LifeTransform protein shakes), skincare (Enummi), and energy support. All formulations center on immune system optimization as the foundation of wellness.

4Life holds exclusive patents on Transfer Factor extraction and combination processes, creating a defensible market position no other network marketing company can replicate with identical technology.
