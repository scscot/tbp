What HC Wellness Offers (in plain English)

HC Wellness (Hispano Club) is a nutritional supplement MLM company based in Mexico City founded by Rafael Chavez Monzon with Pablo Lara serving as CEO. The company emerged around 2019 as a reboot of the original Hispano Club business. HC Wellness markets supplements grouped into Select and Premium product lines. Select Line products ($88) include NAD-Shield, AHCC+ (formulated with shiitake mushroom, anona muricata, and morus alba for immune support), and Connect+ wellness supplements. Premium Line products ($54-$58) include Complex Green nutrition shake, Complex Mocha nutrition shake, and Re-New Pro topical skin gel. The company also offers high-volume product packs reaching $3,000 monthly which reviewers suggest may optimize compensation plan earnings. HC Wellness operates a binary compensation structure paying $210 per pairing of recruited affiliates across binary team sides. The plan includes 13 affiliate ranks from Executive (requiring 2 affiliates with active monthly orders) through Bronze (requiring 15,000 points weekly). Startup requires minimum $200 product purchase for affiliate qualification and commission eligibility.

Due Diligence Notice: HC Wellness has received critical reviews regarding product transparency, unknown supplement origins, aggressive autoship requirements, and recruitment emphasis. The company operates primarily in Spanish-speaking markets with limited English-language information available. Prospective affiliates should thoroughly research product quality, review income disclosure statements, verify supplement sourcing and third-party testing, and understand the binary qualification requirements before joining. Focus on genuine product value delivery rather than recruitment emphasis.

HC Wellness's supplement focus targets Spanish-speaking wellness markets—appeal to Latino health-conscious consumers seeking nutritional support products, immune boosters, and wellness optimization through network marketing distribution model.

How HC Wellness Compensates

HC Wellness operates a binary team compensation plan paying $210 for every pairing of recruited affiliates (one on left leg, one on right leg of binary structure). The plan includes 13 affiliate ranks starting with Executive (recruit 2 affiliates with active monthly product orders), Executive 500 (generate 150 points weekly + maintain 2 affiliates), Executive 1K (250 points weekly), Executive 2K (500 points weekly), Executive 3K (750 points weekly), and Bronze (15,000 points weekly). Higher ranks include Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond levels with increasing team volume requirements. Startup requires minimum $200 product purchase for commission eligibility. Monthly autoship requirements maintain active affiliate status and qualification for binary payouts. The compensation emphasizes team building through binary structure rather than retail customer development, with significant focus on recruiting affiliates who maintain monthly product orders generating points for upline qualification.

Key insight: Binary structure requires balanced team building—affiliates must recruit on both legs equally to maximize $210 pairing bonuses, creating strategic team placement challenges versus unilevel plans allowing natural growth patterns without leg balancing requirements.
