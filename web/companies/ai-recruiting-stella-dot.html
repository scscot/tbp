What Stella & Dot Sells (in plain English)

Stella & Dot is a boutique-style fashion jewelry and accessories company founded in 2003 by Jessica Herrin, celebrating over 20 years of empowering women through flexible entrepreneurship. The product line features contemporary jewelry including layering necklaces, statement earrings, stackable rings, and delicate bracelets with modern vintage aesthetics. Core collections include versatile handbags, fashion scarves, and accessories designed for everyday style and special occasions. Products are crafted with quality materials—gold and silver plating, semi-precious stones, and durable hardware—at accessible price points ($28-$198 range). The brand emphasizes trunk show party experiences where Independent Stylists host intimate shopping events, combining boutique retail atmosphere with social selling. Seasonal collections refresh quarterly with trend-forward designs.

Stella & Dot appeals to women 25-55 seeking affordable luxury jewelry with boutique styling, eliminating the need to visit high-end retail stores while enjoying personalized shopping experiences at home.

Who Buys Stella & Dot

Stella & Dot attracts fashion-conscious women seeking boutique-quality jewelry without department store prices, busy professionals wanting convenient shopping experiences, gift buyers looking for thoughtful presents, and jewelry enthusiasts who appreciate modern vintage designs. The customer base spans women 25-65 with emphasis on 30-50 year old style-aware consumers. The business opportunity appeals to fashion influencers, former retail workers seeking flexibility, stay-at-home moms wanting part-time income, event planners who understand hosting, and entrepreneurial women seeking low-barrier entry business with established brand recognition and proven trunk show model.

Strong appeal to women who value work-life balance and flexible scheduling—host trunk shows around family commitments and personal lifestyle.

How Stella & Dot Distributes

Stella & Dot operates a direct sales compensation plan offering 25-35% commission on personal sales, with higher commission tiers for volume achievers. Independent Stylists earn through trunk show sales, online boutique orders, and team building bonuses. New stylists receive 50% off products during their first 30 days to build inventory and personal collection. The company emphasizes social selling, trunk show parties (in-home or virtual), and digital boutique storefronts. Rank progression includes Stylist, Senior Stylist, Director, Senior Director, and Executive Director levels with increasing team bonuses and leadership incentives. No monthly minimums or inventory requirements—stylists can operate completely on-demand ordering.

Key insight: Trunk show model creates urgency—limited-time shopping experiences with exclusive host rewards drive higher average orders than passive online browsing.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Stella & Dot emphasizes trunk show hosting, styling expertise, and social selling techniques in training. New Independent Stylists learn product knowledge, hosting best practices, and styling consultation skills through online training modules, virtual workshops, and upline mentorship. The company provides trunk show kits, digital marketing assets, and customer engagement tools. However, traditional training focuses on in-person party planning and personal network activation—leaving systematic social media prospecting, automated follow-up sequences, and scalable customer acquisition largely unaddressed.   2. Week 1: Build Your Personal Collection — Use your 50% off first 30 days discount to purchase 8-12 pieces you'll personally wear. Explain host rewards (free jewelry for $500+ party sales, half-price items, exclusive host-only pieces). Set up attractive product displays, create try-on stations, demonstrate styling techniques (layering, mixing metals, statement vs. everyday pieces). Share your personal styling story—why you love wearing Stella & Dot daily. Collect orders, explain auto-ship VIP program (free shipping, exclusive access), and ask for referrals. Follow up within 48 hours with thank-you messages and delivery timelines.
  7. Week 3-4: Customer Reorders & Host Recruitment — Follow up with trunk show attendees 2 weeks after delivery: "How are you loving your new pieces? Want to see the new spring collection?"
