What Soluni Offers (in plain English)

Soluni is a nootropic and biohacking supplement company founded in 2019 and based in Miami, Florida. The company markets smart drugs and cognitive enhancement products through network marketing. Core product lines include Brān (nootropic energy drink with 16 nutrients for brain performance, focus, and mood enhancement using nanotechnology, available in Caramel Macchiato, Chocolate Sea Salt, and Lemon Drop flavors), Zlēm (sleep support supplement with 20+ ingredients for regeneration, recovery, optimization, and fat burning during sleep), Plôs (milk-free thermogenic coffee creamer with weight loss properties), and Uüth (anti-aging gelée in superberry flavor for youthful appearance). Soluni positions products in the biohacking and cognitive enhancement market targeting fitness enthusiasts, productivity seekers, entrepreneurs, and wellness optimizers seeking mental performance advantages. The company operates through Brand Partners earning 10-20% commissions based on rank (Apprentice/Promoter 10%, Influencers 15%, Ambassadors 20%). Qualification requires recruiting 2 affiliates plus maintaining 3 retail customers. Annual registration costs $49.95 providing access to V-Cloud personal storefront on Soluni.com.

Soluni's nootropic focus differentiates from general wellness MLMs—appeal to biohackers, productivity enthusiasts, and cognitive performance seekers wanting competitive mental advantages through smart drug supplementation and brain optimization technology.

How Soluni Compensates

Soluni operates a rank-based compensation structure paying 10% commissions at Apprentice and Promoter levels, 15% at Influencer level, and 20% at Ambassador level. Qualification requires recruiting minimum 2 affiliates and maintaining 3 retail customer orders monthly. First-time member purchases generate 10-20% commissions based on BV (Business Volume). The plan emphasizes both retail customer development and team building through affiliate recruitment. Brand Partners access V-Cloud storefront for direct customer sales. Income potential scales through rank advancement, team volume generation, and customer base expansion. The $49.95 annual registration provides business system access and commission eligibility. The compensation focuses on product movement and customer retention rather than recruitment-only structures, though maintaining affiliate and customer minimums creates ongoing qualification requirements.

Key insight: Nootropic products create potential for repeat customers—users experiencing cognitive benefits reorder consistently, generating residual income from monthly subscriptions versus one-time supplement purchases requiring continuous new customer acquisition.
