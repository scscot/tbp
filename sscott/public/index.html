About Stephen Scott

Stephen Scott is a technology entrepreneur and author who bridges the worlds of artificial intelligence and network marketing. With 14 published books across multiple languages, he helps professionals leverage AI to build thriving businesses and transform lives.

As the creator of Team Build Pro, Stephen has built an AI-powered platform used by network marketers worldwide to manage teams, track growth, and automate communications in English, Spanish, Portuguese, and Tagalog.

His books cover AI implementation, network marketing strategies, personal development, and spirituality. Stephen's unique perspective comes from real-world experience building technology products that solve meaningful problems for entrepreneurs.
