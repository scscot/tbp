What Amare Global Sells (in plain English)

Amare Global is the Mental Wellness Company, offering supplements and products targeting the gut-brain axis—the connection between digestive health and mental well-being. The flagship product is the FundaMentals Pack, a three-product system including MentaBiotics (probiotics for mood support), Energy+ (natural energy and focus), and Amare GBX Fit (weight management with mood support). Additional products include Kids FundaMentals, Amare EDGE (cognitive performance), Sleep+ (restful sleep), and Amare PLUS (antioxidant support).

The brand positions around the tagline "Love Yourself Again" and emphasizes holistic mental wellness through gut health optimization. Amare products target stress management, mood support, cognitive function, energy, and overall emotional resilience. The company uses clinical research, mood tracking systems, and a proprietary Mental Wellness Month campaign to build community around mental health awareness.

Key insight: Mental wellness is personal and often stigmatized. Lead with compassion, community, and solutions—not just supplements.

Markets & Footprint

Amare Global operates in North America, Latin America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and select Middle Eastern markets. Headquarters in Irvine, California, with manufacturing partnerships ensuring GMP-certified production. The company has strong presence in mental health awareness campaigns and partners with wellness influencers globally.

International reach means your messaging must navigate cultural attitudes toward mental health and gut health concepts.

Distribution Model

Amare uses a unilevel compensation plan with up to 10 levels of commissions. Wellness Partners earn retail profits, team volume bonuses, and leadership pools. Ranks progress from Partner to Presidential Diamond with increasing residual income and lifestyle rewards. The company offers a 90-day money-back guarantee and Customer Loyalty Rewards program.

Key insight: Mental wellness storytelling matters. Personal transformation stories drive enrollment better than product specs.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Amare emphasizes mental wellness education through the Amare Academy, mood journal tracking, and community events centered on Mental Wellness Month. New Wellness Partners learn gut-brain axis science through simplified infographics, clinical research summaries, and personal story templates. Training includes social media content creation, mood journey tracking, and compliance around mental health claims. The challenge: discussing mental wellness authentically without crossing into medical or therapeutic claims.
