What Mannatech Sells (in plain English)

Founded in November 1993 by Samuel Caster and headquartered in Flower Mound, Texas, Mannatech is a publicly traded company specializing in nutritional supplements featuring glyconutrients—natural saccharides that support cellular communication for optimal body system function. The flagship product is Ambrotose Complex, a groundbreaking supplement crafted from Manapol premium aloe vera powder and various plant-based glycan ingredients. Beyond Ambrotose, Mannatech offers NutriVerus multivitamins, weight management products, skincare, and wellness supplements designed for immune support, digestive health, cognitive function, and overall vitality. Operating in over 26 countries and trusted by millions, Mannatech is publicly traded on NASDAQ under ticker symbol MTEX.

The glyconutrient science positioning differentiates Mannatech from commodity supplement brands—cellular communication support is more sophisticated than basic vitamin supplementation, appealing to health-conscious consumers seeking advanced nutrition.
