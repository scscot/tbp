What Prüvit Sells (in plain English)

Prüvit is the worldwide leader in ketone technology, specializing in exogenous ketone supplements that put your body into ketosis without strict dietary restrictions. The flagship product line is KETO//OS NAT—the first and only naturally fermented ketone supplement using patent-pending BHB salts that absorb quickly to induce ketosis within 60 minutes of consumption.

Beyond the original KETO//OS, Prüvit offers KETO//OS NAT (naturally fermented with probiotic properties), KETO//OS MAX (30% more ketones and caffeine), and a variety of flavors including Raspberry Lemonade, Maui Punch, Swiss Cacao, Strawberry Peach, and limited-edition seasonal options. Products come in convenient single-serve sachets designed to mix with cold water.

The core value proposition: instant ketosis for energy, mental clarity, appetite suppression, and athletic performance—without the hassle of strict keto dieting.

Markets & Footprint

Prüvit operates globally across the US, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Mexico, Asia (Hong Kong, Malaysia, Singapore), and Europe (United Kingdom, Netherlands, Germany, Italia, Austria). The company serves a diverse customer base from fitness enthusiasts to biohackers to busy professionals seeking sustained energy.

International reach means your recruiting system must work across cultures, languages, and time zones—automation is essential.

Distribution Model

Prüvit uses a multi-level marketing compensation plan that rewards promoters for personal sales and team building. Promoters earn 50% commissions on all personal sales, qualify for Champion Car Bonus ($800/month toward a luxury vehicle at Rank 6), Endorsement Bonus (free products for referring 2 SmartShip customers), and Go MVP Bonus ($500 cash plus accelerated bonuses).

Key insight: The $49 annual promoter fee and low barrier to entry means recruiting volume matters—systematic follow-up wins.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Prüvit emphasizes the 10-Day Drink Ketones Challenge as the primary onboarding tool—providing 8-week programs with celebrity coaches, weekly live video chats, and digital guides. New promoters are encouraged to use social commerce strategies, share personal transformation stories, and build through the "Experience, Share, Build" model. Corporate training includes digital resources, regional events, and promoter community platforms. The challenge: most training focuses on product passion and challenge promotion, not systematic follow-up and objection handling.

Your AI Recruiting Playbook for Prüvit

  1. Lead with lifestyle lanes. Segment prospects into weight loss (keto without restrictions), energy seekers (sustained mental clarity), fitness enthusiasts (endurance and recovery), or biohackers (performance optimization). Your message changes by lane.
  2. Challenge-based onboarding. Use the 10-Day Drink Ketones Challenge as your primary recruiting tool—automate invites, daily check-ins, and experience-sharing prompts with AI follow-up sequences.
  3. Science-backed storytelling. Tie ketone benefits to natural fermentation technology and BHB science. Example: "Our ketones are naturally fermented—the only ones that retain probiotic properties. Here's a 60-second video."
  4. Flavor sampling strategy. When someone expresses interest, offer a flavor variety pack trial. AI tracks day 3, 7, and 14 check-ins with energy/clarity feedback prompts and reorder invitations.
  5. Results tracking system. Ask for daily feedback on energy levels, mental clarity, appetite, and performance—but keep claims compliant. Focus on personal experience, not medical promises.
  6. Community over selling. Position Prüvit as a biohacker community, not a sales pitch. Automate invitations to private Facebook groups, live coaching calls, and team challenges.
  7. Objection scripts ready. Automate responses for: price ("cost per day vs coffee/energy drinks"), MLM concerns ("$49/year tool for health optimization"), competition questions ("link to fermentation technology page").
  8. Duplicate the entire flow. Save your challenge invite sequence, flavor sample follow-ups, science explainer videos, and FAQ responses so new promoters copy it on day one.
