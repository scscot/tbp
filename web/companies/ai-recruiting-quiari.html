What QuiAri Sells (in plain English)

The flagship product line includes QuiAri Shake (meal replacement shakes with maqui berry, available in multiple flavors), QuiAri Energy (energy drink with natural caffeine and maqui antioxidants), and QuiAri Prime (gel packs for on-the-go nutrition and joint support). The product positioning emphasizes convenience, taste, and antioxidant protection through simple daily routines.

The brand story centers on maqui berry's antioxidant power (ORAC score positioning), Chilean harvesting traditions, and taste-first product development. QuiAri emphasizes the fast-start business model with accelerated compensation timing—daily pay features that allow new Promoters to earn commissions quickly. The company uses challenge campaigns (7-day shake challenge, 30-day transformation) and taste-testing events to drive product trial and social sharing. Products target weight management seekers, energy-conscious consumers, busy professionals looking for meal convenience, and business builders attracted to the fast-pay compensation structure.

Key insight: Taste beats science in initial conversations. Lead with flavor preferences and simple routines—then layer in the maqui antioxidant story.

Your AI Recruiting Playbook for QuiAri

  1. Lead with flavor profiling. Segment prospects by taste preferences (chocolate, vanilla, fruity, neutral) and offer customized shake recipe suggestions via AI.
  2. 7-day challenge automation. Create repeatable 7-day shake challenge kits with daily recipe ideas, measurement tracking, and progress celebration messages.
  3. Taste-test event systems. Automate event invitations, reminder sequences, and post-tasting follow-ups with AI-generated personalized messages based on flavor feedback.
  4. Recipe vault organization. Collect shake recipes organized by goal (weight loss, energy, meal replacement, post-workout) with automatic matching to prospect objectives.
  5. Daily routine simplification. Create 60-second videos showing shake preparation, timing suggestions, and integration into morning or afternoon routines—accessible via chatbot.
  6. Objection scripts for skeptics. Pre-script responses to "shakes don't work" with ORAC score data, maqui berry research, and satisfaction guarantee information.
  7. Duplicate the challenge system. Save your 7-day protocols, recipe collections, and event hosting templates so new Promoters can launch identical systems immediately.
