What ASEA Sells (in plain English)

ASEA produces redox signaling supplement products—the first and only shelf-stable form of balanced redox signaling molecules. The flagship product is ASEA Redox Supplement, a liquid supplement designed to support cellular health, immune function, and overall vitality. The product line also includes RENU 28 revitalizing redox gel for skin health, RENU Advanced for intensive skin support, and ASEA VIA product suite (vitamins, omegas, probiotics, energy drinks).

The core story: redox signaling molecules are native to the body and critical for cellular communication, but production declines with age. ASEA's patented process creates stable redox molecules outside the body, supporting the body's natural cellular repair and renewal processes. The positioning targets health-conscious individuals, athletes seeking recovery, and aging populations focused on cellular health.

Key positioning: Cellular health from the inside out—not a symptom fix, but foundational support for the body's natural processes.

Markets & Footprint

ASEA operates globally with presence in North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America. Corporate headquarters in Pleasant Grove, Utah, with manufacturing facilities maintaining strict quality control. The company emphasizes scientific research with partnerships including university studies and athletic endorsements.

International reach means your recruiting must translate cellular biology concepts across cultures and education levels.

Distribution Model

ASEA uses a unilevel compensation plan with up to 8 levels of commissions. Associates earn retail profits, team volume commissions, and leadership bonuses. Ranks progress from Associate to Diamond levels with increasing residual income potential. Monthly autoship programs provide recurring revenue.

Key insight: Science credibility matters. Lead with research, patents, and third-party validation before making income claims.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

ASEA emphasizes education-based marketing through science seminars, third-party research presentations, and athlete testimonials. New Associates learn the redox signaling story through animated videos, cellular biology explanations, and patent documentation. Training includes product sampling protocols, before/after tracking, and compliance around supplement claims. The challenge: explaining redox signaling—a novel concept—without overwhelming prospects or making prohibited health claims.
