What Juice Plus Sells (in plain English)

Juice Plus is a whole-food based nutritional supplement company founded in 1970 and headquartered in Collierville, Tennessee. The flagship products are juice powder concentrate capsules derived from 30+ fruits, vegetables, and berries. The three core blends are Orchard Blend (apple, peach, cranberry, orange, mango, cherry, papaya), Garden Blend (broccoli, kale, cabbage, parsley, tomato, beet, spinach), and Vineyard Blend (grapes, blueberries, raspberries, cranberries, blackberries). Beyond capsules, the product line includes Complete Shakes (plant-based protein with added nutrition), Perform protein bars, and Juice Plus Chewables for children. The Tower Garden aeroponic growing system represents a unique home gardening offering tied to the nutrition message.

Juice Plus positions itself as nutrition insurance for families struggling to eat the recommended servings of produce daily—backed by 40+ peer-reviewed clinical studies on ingredient bioavailability.

Who Buys Juice Plus

Juice Plus attracts health-conscious families, busy professionals seeking convenient nutrition, parents concerned about children's vegetable intake, and fitness enthusiasts looking for plant-based recovery support. The customer base skews toward mothers aged 30-50 who prioritize wellness but lack time for meal prep. The business opportunity appeals to former teachers, nurses, wellness coaches, and stay-at-home parents seeking flexible income without inventory management. The Healthy Starts for Families program offering free products for children creates strong retention and referral momentum.

Strong presence in North America and Europe with growing markets in Asia and Australia.

How Juice Plus Distributes

Juice Plus operates a unilevel compensation plan with seven levels of depth plus generational backend bonuses. Partners earn retail commissions up to 6% on personal sales after hitting volume targets, plus network marketing commissions starting at 90 PV monthly qualification. Residual commissions are paid through the unilevel structure based on team volume. Ranks include Partner, Senior Partner, Sales Coordinator, through National Marketing Director. The $50 enrollment fee keeps barrier to entry low. Unique advantage: the Healthy Starts program gives free children's products when adults order, creating built-in referral mechanisms and family retention.

Key insight: Clinical research backing makes credibility easier than most nutrition MLMs—lean into the science, not hype.

Training & Onboarding Snapshot

Juice Plus emphasizes personal experience and clinical research in training. New partners are encouraged to use products for 90 days before recruiting, attend virtual training sessions, and leverage corporate-provided study summaries. The company provides scripts for health screening questionnaires and follow-up protocols. However, traditional training relies heavily on home parties, wellness presentations, and one-on-one meetings—leaving systematic digital prospecting and automated follow-up largely unaddressed. Most partners struggle with consistent lead generation and duplicating their approach across a geographically dispersed team.
